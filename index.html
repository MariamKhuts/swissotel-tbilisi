ACCOMMODATION OVERVIEW

Swissotel Tbilisi offers a 5-star living experience in the heart of city Revel in five-star comfort when you select a spacious guest room at Swissotel Tbilisi. Whether you stay with us for one night or one week, we invite you to make the most of our Vitality offering.

Wake up to views of old Tbilisi, Dry Bridge, Mtatsminda Mountain and breathtaking views. Revel in five-star comfort when you select a spacious guest room at Swissotel Tbilisi. Our diverse accommodation offers our discerning guests unique experiences, such as rare city vistas and private terraces. Common to all, however, is an authentic dedication to combining the warmth of a Georgian welcome with precise, contemporary Alpine design features. Enjoy comfort-enhanced beds and exquisitely upholstered divans. Our bathrooms allow you to revitalize and refresh, while high-speed internet and spacious desks help business travelers stay connected. Swissotel Tbilisi features 130 rooms, including Swiss Business Advantage, Swiss Business Advantage Twin, Swiss Vitality, Deluxe King, Suite and Presidental Suite Rooms. Welcome to a room that offers more than just a place to sleep, shower and take advantage of the Wi-Fi. Our rooms revitalizes body, mind and soul by focusing on maximizing your comfort.
